Add the ObsidianOS-repo to your raspberry pi!

Open terminal and paste this into it, wget -O - http://oxmc.github.io/ObsidianOS/files/addrepo.sh | bash

Now paste, sudo apt-get install ob-imager to get the latest ObsidianOS-Imager on your raspberry pi!
